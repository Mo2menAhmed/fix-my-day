# Fix My Day Feedback Form

Ask these four questions after each tester uses the app for 10 to 15 minutes.

## 1. First 20 seconds

What did you think Fix My Day does, and what did you think you should tap first?

## 2. Best moment

Which screen, phrase, plan, or action felt most helpful or calming?

## 3. Biggest friction

Where did you feel confused, unconvinced, bored, judged, or unsure what to do next?

## 4. Would you reuse it?

Would you open this app on a real bad day? Why or why not, and which premium benefit would be most tempting later?

## Arabic language follow-up

If the tester used Arabic or Egyptian Arabic: did the copy feel natural, clear, and trustworthy? Which phrase or screen should be rewritten?
