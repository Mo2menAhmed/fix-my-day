# Google Play Store Listing Draft

## App Title

Fix My Day

## Short Description

Calm recovery plans for stressful, messy, or unproductive days.

## Full Description

Fix My Day helps you recover the next useful part of a hard day.

When the morning is wasted, the room is messy, studying feels impossible, work is slipping, or sleep was bad, Fix My Day gives you a small reset plan you can actually do.

Choose what went wrong, pick how much time you have, and follow a calm plan with:

- A quick win to start immediately
- Practical timed steps
- A simple checklist
- A focus timer
- A short reflection to save what helped
- Local history so you can repeat useful resets

Fix My Day supports English and Arabic. Your progress stays on your device. No account is required.

Current reset categories include:

- Wasted the morning
- Feeling overwhelmed
- Cannot start studying
- Workday rescue
- Need to clean my desk or room
- Bad sleep recovery
- Before bed reset
- Tomorrow planning

Fix My Day is not medical care, therapy, or professional advice. It is a practical self-management tool for everyday recovery, planning, and focus.

## Privacy Policy Text Draft

Fix My Day respects your privacy.

Fix My Day does not require an account and does not use a backend server for app content or user progress.

The app stores your preferences and progress locally on your device, including:

- selected language
